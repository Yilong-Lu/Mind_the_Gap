Example 1:

Two people take turns jumping rope. Each person jumps for 1 minute, and the winner is the one who jumps the most.

Task Name: 
Two-person Jump Rope Contest

Task Description:
Two players take turns jumping rope. Each player jumps for 1 minute, and the goal is to see who can jump more times.

Detailed Task Setup:
Both participants stand in a designated area. One player jumps while the other counts the jumps. After the first player finishes, the second player takes their turn.

Task Goal:
The goal is to see who can jump the most times within the time limit.

Scoring System:
Each successful jump counts as 1 point. The player with the most points wins.


Example 2:

A person takes a peaceful walk by the lake, enjoying the scenery and relaxing.

Task Name:
Walking by the Lake

Task Description:
The participant takes a walk along the lake to relax and unwind.

Detailed Task Setup:
The participant walks along the lake, enjoying the scenery and the calm atmosphere. No other objects are involved, just the peaceful environment of the lake.

Task Goal:
The goal is to relax and clear the mind while enjoying the walk.

Scoring System:
The participant can rate their relaxation level on a scale of 1-10 after the walk, with 10 being fully relaxed and 1 being not relaxed at all.
